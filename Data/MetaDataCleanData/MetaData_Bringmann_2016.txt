Data format: .csv file 
6329 Rows, 11 Columns 

Variables:
-ID: participant id number
-day: Number describing the day of observation
-beep: Order of observations on a single day
-observationID: Unique ID corresponding with the order of observations
-missing: codes missing observation. 0 for recorded, 1 for missing, 2 for partially
-Angry: Observation on a scale ranging from 1-100. Recoded from: KWAAD
-Depressed: Observation on a scale ranging from 1-100. Recoded from: DEPRE
-Sad: Observation on a scale ranging from 1-100. Recoded from: DROEV
-Anxious: Observation on a scale ranging from 1-100. Recoded from: ANGST
-Relaxed: Observation on a scale ranging from 1-100. Recoded from: ONTSP
-Happy: Observation on a scale ranging from 1-100. Recoded from: BLIJ
