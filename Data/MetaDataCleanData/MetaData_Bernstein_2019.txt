Author Data: Bernstein et al. (2019) 

Citation: Bernstein, E. E. , Curtiss, J. E. , Wu, G. Y. , Barreira, P. J. & McNally, R. J. (2019). Exercise and Emotion Dynamics. Emotion, 19 (4), 637-644. doi: 10.1037/emo0000462.

Label in code: Bernstein_2019
Label in text: Bernstein 2019

Data format: .csv file 
5703 Rows, 9 Columns 

Variables:
-ID: participant id number
-day: Number describing the day of observation
-beep: Order of observations on a single day
-observationID: Unique ID corresponding with the order of observations
-missing: codes missing observation. 0 for recorded, 1 for missing, 2 for partially
-Cheerful: Observation on a scale ranging from 0-100
-Anxious: Observation on a scale ranging from 0-100. Recoded from "Fear"
-Content: Observation on a scale ranging from 0-100
-Sad: Observation on a scale ranging from 0-100
