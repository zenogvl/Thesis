Author Data: Bar Kalifa et al. (2020)

Citation: Eran Bar-Kalifa & Haran Sened (2020) Using Network Analysis for Examining Interpersonal Emotion Dynamics, Multivariate Behavioral Research, 55:2, 211-230, DOI: 10.1080/00273171.2019.1624147

Label in code: Bar_2020_DS1
Label in text: Bar Kalifa 2020 study 1

Data format: .csv file 
5530 Rows, 6 Columns 

Variables:
-ID: participant id number
-day: Number describing the day of observation
-beep: Order of observations on a single day
-observationID: Unique ID corresponding with the order of observations
-missing: codes missing observation. 0 for recorded, 1 for missing, 2 for partially 
-Anxiety: Observation on a scale ranging from 1-5. Recoded from: Anx
-Sad: Observation on a scale ranging from 1-5. Recoded from: Sad
-Vigor: Observation on a scale ranging from 1-5. Recoded from: Vig
-Contentment: Observation on a scale ranging from 1-5. Recoded from: Con
